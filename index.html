Infinity Stones

In case you don’t care for all the Marvel movies, you can check out this smaller article specifically talking about journey of each of the stones.

5 Stones

    1. Power Stone

    • Power Stone gives the wielder super strength.

    Soul Stone

    Soul Stone gives the ability to manipulate the soul and resurrect the dead.

    Time Stone

    Time Stone gives the power to control time.

    Mind Stone

    Mind Stone gives the power to control minds.

    Reality Stone

    Aether is the Reality Stone. Unlike other stones, this is in the form of a viscous liquid and changes shape. It gives the wielder the power to control realities.

About:

A long time before the beginning of the first Captain America movie, a relic called the Tesseract made its way to Earth.
The best guess would be that Odin, Thor’s father, King of Asgard, left it on Earth for safekeeping.
This contains the Space Stone (which gives the power to create wormholes) but we’ll refer to it as the Tesseract.
